RomaJS + PUG Roma + Special Guest

22/7/2016 - 17:00:00

For the third anniversary of RomaJS community, this month we will have a unified meetup with another big, backend focused, community in Rome: PUG Roma. Special guest on this meetup will be Gilles Rupert, founder of RomaJS and javascript developer at Facebook.

Agenda:

19:15 - Happy Birthday RomaJS!

19:20 - Keynote by Gilles Rupert from Facebook: Q&A Session

19:50 - Once upon a time, there were css, js and server-side rendering by @JellyBellyDev (https://twitter.com/jellybellydev) and @MatteoManchi (https://twitter.com/MatteoManchi)

20:40 - Dinner to celebrate the 3rd anniversary of RomaJS
