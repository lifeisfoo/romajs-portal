RomaJS December Meetup: Seneca & Microservices

29/12/2015 - 18:30:00

ATTENZIONE: L'ascensore di Via Giolitti, 34 è inutilizzabile. L'appuntamento è all'ingresso dentro termini. Scriveteci quando arrivate e scendiamo ad aprirvi. Scusate il disagio

During the last few years the microservices movement has grown more and more, are we entering a new era of software development? What is a microservice and what's the complexity behind this new approach? A small walk through on microservices and an introduction of Seneca, a microservices toolkit for Node.js.

Bring your computer for live coding for the last meetup of the year!
